Cam Coulter is a little softie who likes to meditate over the word “indiepunk.” They think incessantly about ethical technology, speculative fiction, and intentional community and will talk your head off about digital accessibility and the commons if you let them. They write about science fiction and fantasy for Skiffy and Fanty, and they blog about social justice, simple living, community, and spirituality at The Ruined Report. Their poetry has appeared in Eternal Haunted Summer, Eye to the Telescope, and Polu Texni.
